About me

Finance and BI Analyst based in London

Born and raised in South Africa, I moved to London in September 2016.

I am CIMA qualified with ten years work experience in both Finance and Data Analytics. I am a highly analytical, ambitious individual who loves taking on new challenges and learning new skills.

My newest challenge has been learning how to code. Learning skills like CSS, HTML, Javascript and much more. I have really enjoyed this challenge and looking forward to working on more projects.

My passions outside of work include cooking, travelling, reading and spending time with people. My dream one day would be to open up my own Guest House where I could combine my passions for people and cooking together. I would host cooking classes, wine tasting and so much more.
